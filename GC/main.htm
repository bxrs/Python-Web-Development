Traits such as Accessories, Colour, Hair,
Mouth, Eyes and Background are sorted into rarities.
Common, Rare, Super Rare and Legendary are the four
levels of rarity in our NFT's that are randomly
generated onto our Giraffes, adjusting the value
and rarity of the Giraffe. Out of all our Giraffes,
twenty five are hand-drawn and completely unique
from the rest, be on the look-out for them.

The Giraffe Circle is a collection of one
thousand unique and randomly generated
NFT's that are placed on the Ethereum Blockchain.
Giraffe Circle was created in October and will
launch the NFT's later in the year on Opensea.
A whitelist will be available before release under
certain requirements, stay tuned to keep updated.
The Giraffe Circle was created by a team of one person.
Minting will not be available along with whitelisting for this
project, all NFT's will be released on the same date
and same time, a release date will be publicly announced
soon, keep and eye out!
